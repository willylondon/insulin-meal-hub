🍽️ Insulin-Smart Meal Hub

Mobile web app for daily insulin-sensitive meal suggestions + ebook data collection

📱 How to Use the App

  1. Open on your phone: Navigate to the app folder and open index.html in your mobile browser
  2. Daily suggestions: App shows lunch & dinner meals tailored for insulin sensitivity
  3. View recipes: Tap "View Recipe" for full ingredients and instructions
  4. Log meals: Tap "I Ate This" to track what you actually eat
  5. Export data: Use "Export Ebook Data" to get JSON for your ebook analysis

🚀 Launch the App

🔓 Make Your Google Sheet Public (Required)

The app needs to read your meal data from Google Sheets. Here's how to make it public:

  1. Open your Google Sheet: Sheet Link
  2. Click the "Share" button (top-right corner)
  3. Click "Change to anyone with the link"
  4. Set permission to "Viewer" (not "Editor")
  5. Click "Copy link" and "Done"
✅ Why this is safe: The app only reads data, cannot edit your sheet. Your meal logs are stored locally on your device.

📊 Ebook Data Collection

This app automatically collects data for your future ebook:

  • Meal frequency: Which recipes you actually prepare most often
  • Preparation patterns: Average prep time, difficulty ratings
  • Insulin benefit themes: Which benefits are most relevant to your journey
  • Personal adaptations: Your notes and modifications to recipes

Export your data anytime as JSON for analysis in Google Sheets, Excel, or data visualization tools.

➕ Adding More Recipes

Two ways to expand your meal database:

Option 1: Direct in Google Sheets

  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Add new rows in the "Recipes" sheet
  3. Follow the same column format as existing recipes
  4. The app will automatically load new recipes

Option 2: Edit the Fallback Data

If Google Sheets isn't working, edit fallback-data.json and add new meal objects.

⚠️ Troubleshooting

App shows "Loading meals..." forever? Your Google Sheet is still private. Follow Step 2 above.

Can't access on mobile? Upload the folder to GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any web host.

Data not saving? Make sure your browser allows local storage (all modern browsers do).

🚀 Advanced: Deploy to Web

For easier mobile access, deploy to free hosting:

GitHub Pages (Free)

  1. Create GitHub account (if you don't have one)
  2. Create new repository: insulin-meal-hub
  3. Upload all files from the insulin-meal-hub folder
  4. Go to Settings → Pages → Select "main" branch → Save
  5. Your app will be at: https://[username].github.io/insulin-meal-hub

Netlify (Easiest)

  1. Go to Netlify Drop
  2. Drag and drop the entire insulin-meal-hub folder
  3. Get a free URL like https://your-meal-hub.netlify.app
